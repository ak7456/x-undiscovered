➪ Born of Time-nomad

There will be more and more of ‘Time Nomads’ in the future who live through their own time instead of the typical notion of time. Since people live through their own individual time it is difficult to define what ‘time exactly is and is almost impossible to find any touchpoint with others nowadays in changing time.

So we are proposing Tiki, a product that can help people to be aware of each other’s schedule and make easier appointments with people around us.

You can edit a time card on the home screen. Prints quickly and easily in app.

TIKI’s mobile application service provides a way to edit cards of data connected to the scheduler. It also helps build and deliver scheduling and expansion services between users.

 

➪ New Paradigm of Communication Tools

To solve this complex problem, we have introduced a new concept of communication tools. The TIKI Service provides a device for output and creates a time card. With a portable-sized printing machine that fits your bag, you can introduce yourself anytime, anywhere and share time information with each other.

In addition, the app connected to the device allows you to design cards quickly and easily. After card exchange, the app offers a variety of services, including schedule sharing and comparative analysis, enabling more efficient communication.

 

➪ Beyond the only identity

The current business card concept is suitable to represent a single identity. In the future, there will be creative and changeable users, not even a single job. Time cards are a means of free expression and quick delivery method of these various identities.

It also briefly displays calendar information with the user’s identity information. Timeline templates such as Daily, Weekly, etc. can be used to express time information on a variety of topics. It helps you communicate effectively with others when you change everyday!

 

Carry a portable TIKI Device, and Tags the product with the cellphone. You can edit a time card on the home screen. Prints quickly and instantly with apps.

 

➪ TIKI as a device

TIKI is thermal printer that can print time cards. TIKI is designed with a motif from the behavior of using a wallet when carrying a business card.

The size is similar to that of a conventional wallet that can be easily carried, and the way insert thermal paper is similar to insert business card to wallet. Press the lid on the top of the product to pop out the slot where you can insert the card.

In the front part of the product, there is an output part that prints out, and in the back part, there is a LED to indicator the device’s lack of thermal paper and the charging status. TIKI is wireless chargeable. If you put it on the wireless charging tray of the same size as the main body and then charging starts.
