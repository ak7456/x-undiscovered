X: Undiscovered

We call unknown objects ‘X’. We place an X on things that are far from 'familiarity' and face it as if we solve algebra problems. The 'X' sign clearly helps human to judge and reveals the boundary of knowing and not knowing. It allows you to confirm where you are, and it also works as an indicator of which X to remove next.

Now we are on the verge of the next unexplored world 'X', which suddenly came under the name of New-Normal. This 'unknown' world, which has not yet been fully revealed, presents us with both fear and expectation. What order and value are flowing in this new world, hidden by an unknown veil?

 

2020 MCP <X: Undiscovered> is an exhibition which presents humankind taking a step foward towards uncovering the identity of World X. We will draw a new map in a world that is not the same as before, a mixture of concept and values.

Through this exhibition, we carefully define the new world called X and suggest future designs to work there. We hope that our discovery will be a clue to dispel fears of tomorrow that we've never experienced before.

 

The Value of X

The 'X' in the main graphic of this exhibition symbolizes the unstable and constantly changing New Normal era. New values found by 8 teams are expressed as an transparent object to represent the infinite possibility of each category.

Also, a hypothesis was used to describe the X world. There are

5 hypotheses: Untact Love, Nature Revive, Edible Fashion, Hand Coat, and Social Distance. These future hypotheses conflicts with the values that were familiar in the past. By seeing these unfamiliar hypotheses, you can prepare yourself into the new future, X.

Exhibition Space

Chapter 1.

BEHOLD

"BEHOLD" is the starting point for entering the unknown "SITE X" and welcomes viewers to the first space of the exhibition. In the main space, viewers begin to learn about X with curiosity about SITE X. The central installation allows viewers to observe the attempt to explore X over the layer, previewing the many X studies proceeded by eight teams. There are 5 hypotheses for describing the X world. You can prepare yourself into the future before entering 'Site X' where there are new future products and services.

 

Chapter 2.

Head to X

"HEAD TO X" is a transitional space towards where viewer walk towards "X". The huge boards on both sides of the passage remind you of the walls of X's lab, and viewers can discover the elements of X expanding and intermingling. "HEAD TO X" is a bridge space for going to "SITE X", also an inquiry process of the eight teams.

Chapter 3.

Site X

Now people are recognizing new discoveries that can be bought and sold. "SITE X" is a new space that sells experiments of the next world "X" that suddenly comes under the name of New Normal. Discover new value through eight teams.
