_> Safe Zone,

the most reliable space

COVID-19 has increased anxiety about outside activities. However, due to the reality that going out is completely unstop-pable, people's dependence has increased on the 'inside the house' as the most personal and reliable space, which is the 'safe zone'.

We want home to be the safest place when we return from outside activities that have makes us anxious all day long.

_> Where your first steps

are headed

Home is a space that gives us comfort. The moment we enter the house and feel comfortable, we recognize that we have entered the house entirely.

Before COVID-19, we were able to feel comfortable just by taking off our shoes at the front door. However, in the new normal era, we can only be relieved after stopping by the bathroom and washing our hands. Now, returning from the outside and heading to the bathroom has become a natural flow to let go of the anxiety outside the house and come into the house completely.

After entering the house through this process, they moved to a place where they could enjoy their own room, kitchen or living room.

OASE will become an oasis in the home where users can safely hear during their journey to the Safe Zone.

_> OASE, oasis in my home

We head to the bathroom with leaving our items and luggage that were used outside on the table along the path. And The space that we go through for a while before washing our hands, we saw this as an 'oasis'.

In an 'oasis' that goes through a long and difficult journey, travelers tighten their necks and tidy up their surroundings, after they relieve their anxiety and gain the strength to travel to their destination.

 ‘Things that you spent a day outside with me.’ If you touch it again after washing your hands, the important act of ‘washing’ becomes meaningless, and products that are not cared for will eventually contaminate the entire house, including yourself.

OASE will make the space for a perfect oasis through hygiene care of your belongings. After washing our hands, we will be able to enter completely “in the house” with our cleaned belongings. As such, OASE will become an oasis in the home where users can safely go through during their journey to the Safe Zone.

The functions, shapes, and products of handle thins carried by each person are not determined, and there are countless variations depending on the situation, weather, and condition. Based on this, a new brand language was established by converting the alphabet into shapes that are not standardized.

_> OASE, containing the

mood of an oasis

The oasis is drawn to us in a mystical form through the images of the sand breeze, the wave of springs, the sparkle of the intense sunlight, and the shade of the palm trees.

'OASE' brings a new mood to the house by expressing an oasis through materials.

When viewed from a distance, the sand-colored translucent body and the sterilizing parts in between are remind of an oasis seen through the gentle wind of sand. In addition, the main sterilization part appears to be floating in the air, adding a mysterious feel.

When you get closer, the glass top with a straight pattern creates a sway according to the viewing angle and light changes. The waves of this light express the waves of the oasis and the intense sunlight.

'OASE' brings a new mood to the house by expressing an oasis through materials.

_> Perfect care without

blind spots

OASE uses UV-C LEDs for complete hygiene care without blind spots.

When the sterilization-care mode is activated so that all sides of your belongings can be cared without blind spots, uv-c LEDs are emitted simultaneously from the top and bottom. The LED from the top cares the top and side of the belongings, and the LED comes out only in the area where the belongings are placed below, intensively sterilizing the bottom of the belongings.

In addition, by designing as you place your belongings on the flesh, the area that your belongings can’t reach is minimized, and the sanitary blind spot at the bottom of your belongings has been eliminated.

 

_> Design for user's safety

OASE designed the scenario to prevent users from facing uv-c LED. 5 seconds after the user puts all the belongings to be sterilized, the uv-c LED turns on and the sterilization-care mode starts. The personal belongings are sterilized during the average time of 1 minute and 30 seconds for the user to wash their hands, after which the white LED is turned on and the device goes into standby mode. When the user leaves the bathroom, they are faced with the sterilized belonging things.

In addition, the flesh structure was designed so that the uv-c LED does not directly touch the user's field of view. The LED from the bottom comes only from the area where the belongings are raised, and the LED from the flesh is not directly visible due to the gap between the flesh. The glass top plate prevents the LEDs coming from the top and bottom from passing directly to the user, making it safer to use.

 

 

The structure of non-eye detacted UV-C LED is designed as esthetic shapes.

_> Sensuous confidence

through cooling air

The sterilization care using the conventional uv-c LED did not feel any changes before and after the care, which did not give users confidence.

OASE compensates for this with cooling-air. The cooling-air that comes out after sterilization cools the electronic products, and it makes you sense that your belongings are clean through refreshing like the cool breeze that blows when you open a window.

After sterilization, cooling air awaken

the cleaness sensibly.

_> Safe mode for loving family

OASE considered the safety of small children and pets who could see the LEDs from above due to their low eye level.

If motion is detected around the product during the sterilization-care mode through the motion detection sensor, sterilization is paused and the uv-c led stops. If the movement goes away and no movement is detected for more than 5 seconds, the sterilization care mode is activated again.
