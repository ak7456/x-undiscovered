_> Discovery of the Non-physical Space

Currently we live in a new space surrounded by various devices from smartphones to tablet PCs.  From your boss’s texts to messages from friends, endless notifications are packed into life. 

Whenever and wherever we are, the place we are in can become either a place for work or rest.  We don’t need an office, and we can’t fully escape from the office.  The tablet PC used for watching videos on Netflix and YouTube is also used for work, and reports are sent via Instagram and KakaoTalk.

Through trends like BYOD, our devices have mixed uses in work and daily life.  A life without separating work and rest can feel tiresome because work seems to carry on endlessly.

‘pivot’ constructs an axis for the non-physical space inside smartphones and tablet PCs.

 

pivot is a service that separates work from rest through the link between tablet PC and smartphone.

Separate your work from your life through a pivot.

_> Coming to and Leaving Work

‘pivot’ provides a standard for division to a life where work and rest are entangled.

Until now, work took place in the office, and rest was for home.  However, in a work-from-home environment, work and rest feels as if they were not separated.  We thought of the life where work and rest was divided by moving to and from office and home.

Open up the product for a workspace fit for concentration, and turn it over to find a place for wholesome relaxation.  Smartphones become the card for checking in and out of work.  Placing it on the product starts up the workplace and putting it down carries you away from work to rest.  By sorting mixed notifications into those related to work or daily life, a new axis for space is constructed.

Tag your phone in, you can go to work and leave the office as soon as you take it off.

Separate your work from your life through a pivot.

_> pivot's Device

The product creates an environment fit for concentrating on work and rest.  It will help us to better utilize the BYOD we own.

In the task mode, an environment for efficient working is provided.  Attach a tablet PC to use it as a station for carrying out the work, and a smartphone to use it as a hub for managing work. The keyboard exists as a source to input information. These three elements interact intimately to help concentrate on finishing work efficiently.

In the play mode, an environment fit for concentrating on sound resting appears.  The tablet PC changes to accom-modate the enjoyment of media content.  The keyboard from task mode disappears, and the smartphone is disconnected to be used for daily life instead of for work.

 

Converted to task mode if opened or to play mode when turned.

Manage works in the task box more efficiently.

_> pivot's Service

The smartphone manages the work, and also marks the the start and end of work as it makes contact with the product.  Notifica-tions related to work are separated from others and put into the task box, and offered when you get to work.  The task boxes organize what you need to do in a smart way.  They connect with programs used for work, and share informa-tion with the tablet PC to help finish getting the work done.  Put the smartphone down to get off from work, and you can use it to enjoy daily life again.

The tablet PC is used to enjoy daily life and also to get work done.  When you start working via smartphone, it brings up what you’ve been working on previously.  The axis changes from rest to work to provide a working environment.  Select what you need to do from the task box in the smartphone, and you can work on it on the tablet PC.  When you get off from work, the Tablet PC’s screen changes back to fit your daily needs, and becomes fit for enjoying media content.

Create an axis that separates the notice in the smartphone into work and life.

Manage works in the task box more efficiently.

_> The Intimate Interaction Experience

Intimate interactions centering on the product take place between smartphone, tablet PC, and keyboard to help efficient working.  Create an organic work environment by taking in notifications from the smartphone, connecting to programs, and sending files to the tablet PC by using NFC.  There is no need to keep looking at your KakaoTalk and mail inbox, or sticking notes to your PC screen.  Also, the keyboard is charged by your smartphone’s powershare, thus providing a smooth user experience without needing other charging methods.

With a difference from task mode, play mode enables people to concentrate on sound resting.  Put away the keyboard and notifica-tions from work, and watch movies and shows at a comfortable location with your mind at ease.  Set it up wherever you are, and just enjoy.

Through a clear division between work and rest, we actively lead our own lives.

 

Focus more on your work with the interaction of your tablet PC and smartphone.

_> A New Space Needs a New Axis

A division between work and rest is not visible inside the invisible space inside smartphones and tablet PCs.  Waking up and looking at the notifications can make us feel tired before the day starts.

‘pivot’ actively constructs a new axis in a new space.  Work is efficiently carried out in the task space, and ideas for concentrating on resting are provided in the play space.  Open it up, flip it around to create a space to fit the situation, and use a smartphone to check in and out of work.  Through the axis you create, you can lead a wiser life.

‘pivot’ dreams of us leading our own lives.
