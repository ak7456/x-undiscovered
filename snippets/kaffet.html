_> Spontaneity, Fading Away

Sharing time and space together is something we used to take for granted. Now, with COVID-19 widespread throughout the globe, various cultural and social events based on physical gathering are losing their light. Busking (street performance) is not an exception as its essence is in the spontaneous joy of running into a performer on the street. As social gathering turned into social distancing, are we no longer able to listen to the romantic music of buskers?

What if we could still run into buskers’ music without having to share the same time and space?

_> The Reinterpretation of

Spontaneity

We have reached the New-Normal Era. Rather than waiting for our lives before COVID-19 to come back, we should reinterpret the true essence of busking: joy of running into a musical performance unexpectedly.

So we present Kaffet, a location-based online busking platform where you can still unexpectedly encounter the performances buskers had left behind for someone like you. "Catch the music, listen to the space!"

_> kaffet, Identity

kaffet

The name Kaffet was inspired by the idea of a carpet that symbolizes a heartwarming stage prepared for a listener and a busker. Kaffet’s color scheme is based on various red colors as we wish Kaffet to be the red carpet for the buskers.

 

Main graphic motif

‘Circle’ is a metaphor that embraces both buskers’ music and listeners’ enjoyment. The design philosophy behind the oval shapes springs from projecting the Circle with a perspective view that projects a ‘Space.’ As the Circle meets the Space, a listener and a busker can still share the spontaneous joy without having to share both time and space.

 

Listener

When the Circles of buskers’ music sit side by side, the negative space created by the circles is a star shape that stands for a ‘Listener.’

 

Pick

When the Circles overlap with each other, the space in the middle shared by all the Circles becomes a guitar pick. The Pick is used as a currency within Kaffet.

 

LOGO & Typeface

The Expanded Font adds visual consistency to Kaffet’s brand by harmonizing with its signature oval graphic.

_> kaffet, Spontaneity of the

New-Normal Era

1) Buskers can pin their live broadcast to any location as they begin the show. Only those who pass through the certain radius of the pin will receive the broadcast.

2) Upon the spontaneous encounter with a busker, you will pop on the screen as a Star that illuminates the busker’s performance. Your Star will sparkle as you leave a comment. The Pick is there for you in case you want to share your joy with the busker.

3) kaffet also has a SuperPin feature that helps you spread your favorite busker’s performance. Through SuperPinning your favorite performance, you can advertise your favorite busker to the other listeners near you.

4) Even when the show is over, the music is not over. The music will remain in the same place, getting automatically updated on the playlists of those who pass by. kaffet and its spontaneous performances will provide a special moment to your day.

5) The audio clips from the buskers of the area you passed by will be waiting for you in your playlist.

_> kaffet’s Proposal for the

New Normal Era

With kaffet, we can truly live the New-Normal era as we enjoy busking without being bound to the traditional idea of time and space.

It’s time for us to move on from the times we used to desire social gatherings and suggest a new way to lead our daily lives in the New Normal era where we have to keep social distances.

We hope kaffet, the location-based music platform will inspire you in your journey of surviving the New Normal era.
