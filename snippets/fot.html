_> The evolution of

New normal’s tools

Historically, unexpected outbreaks such as war, global warming, epidemic break downs of our daily lives has brought us New Normal. Repeating the structure of ‘Normal-Outbreak-New Normal’ maintains the society, which is similar with the evolution of ‘DNA’ that is to adapt to changing environments. At this point, the most important role is mutation. Mutations, which are the basic components of evolution, have the potential to properly cope with emergencies in society. They are essential for maintaining and sustaining it.

In emergencies, tools of mutation are used according to the occasion. Tool is a word that has purpose, unlike ’things’. New Normal’s tools evolves by being used, transformed, and utilized unlike original purposes.

_> Telecommuting

After the pandemic, telecommuting, which spread as an appropriate form of work in a non-contact society, has brought many changes. The key characteristic of telecommuting is an ambiguous boundary between work space and private space, unlike existing work environments. After all, in a house with limited physical movement, workers feel a lot of fatigue from successive work processes. They need a conscious separation of work and relaxation.

 

There is a driving-through corona inspection station that appeared after COVID-19. Originally, the 'driving through' which is method of shopping in a car is utilized as a corona test in the non-face-to-face society of New Normal.

_> Housework And

Refreshment

We've found a mutant moment where telecommuters were doing unproductive things to be productive at work. When they lose their concentration in the middle of work, they hang out the laundry or vacuum for a while to ventilate.

Cleaning is a troublesome thing. However, someone else feels psychologically satisfied by organizing things and cleaning up the surroundings. We want to expand that experience. If you have to do housework anyway, why don’t you do it in a more pleasant, satisfying, and comfortable way with ‘fot’?

 

_> Housework And Refresh

1. Refresh by editing and changing the surrounding environment

2. Refresh by playing with

3. Refresh through change of senses

4. Refresh through physical activity

5. Refresh by relaxing

We put people's desire to refresh in the product so that users naturally feel the housework as a refreshment. The shapes and interactions of the products all metaphorize the fresh moments of everyday life. Telecommuters can take a walk with a vacuum cleaner inside the house just like going for a walk with their pets. It also ventilates the air and refreshes the atmosphere as if drinking a cup of tea.

_> A station managing the

atmosphere, Editor

‘Station’ is Editor's charging station, and at the same time, it functions as a switch and timer that can be used during work. ‘Switch mode’ is a mode to change function. Insert the Editor into the station and rotate it 90-degrees clockwise from the regular position. After that, it will connect with IoT products such as the color temperature of the lighting and the opening and closing of the blinds, which is related to the home environment. So it distinguishes work and rest by changing the environment. It can be used as a ‘timer mode’ when rotated more than 90 degrees from the regular position, and the environment is maintained in work mode for as long as the user wants.

The circular handle acts as an editor for fot,

and interacts with the user using three axes.

Feel a variety of refreshments through Switching, Pushing, and Rubbing.

_> Take a walk in the

house, Vaccy

Induce adequate physical activity through cleaning and feel refreshed while playing with Vaccy. Vaccy is a wireless vacuum cleaner designed with the motif of refreshing by walking with a dog.

In the form of a neckless type in the existing wireless vacuum cleaner, the user can control the device with the ‘Editor’ to clean the part carefully. When paired with the Editor, it lights up through the LED indicator of the vacuum cleaner, and the Vaccy moves according to the handle.

 

_> Mood change

like a cup of tea, Pot

Pot is a fog machine that helps antibacterial and refreshing by vaporizing phytoncide oil. Pot is designed with the motif of a teacup. When the pot is operated, the aroma scent and smoke are fumed from the top of the product and then the telecommuter can sensorially refresh by feeling the space change. Open the lid of the product, add phytoncide oil and water, and then combine the editor on the top and turn it to adjust the smoke intensity and usage time.
