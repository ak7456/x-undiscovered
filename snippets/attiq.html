_> Our changed lives

Many changes have occurred in our lives since the corona virus(COVID-19). The realm of 'home' is more valuable than ever and has become an important nest of our own. Then, what kind of life are we living in a situation where we are disconnected from the outside? As the time spent at home with our family increases, people begin to bring outside experiences into their homes.

My room becomes a bedroom or a home office, and the living room becomes a home café, and sometimes it becomes a fitness room, which is no longer special. If this type of life becomes a daily routine, the needs for external experiences will be more subdivided in the New Normal era, and the 'fluidity' of spaces will become even more important.

 

_> X: Restroom

Among the spaces in the house, the bathroom is a space where it reflects a lot of lifestyles. The toilet is a common space, but it is also a space that is used as a complete private space. When seeing a range of users using the bathroom, people read books, or take a shower or look at a tablet PC. Or they use a cell phone, while taking a half-bath and drinking wine. Therefore, the current toilets reflect the lifestyles of various users. Looking at how each person use the toilet in his or her own way, we discovered an insight that New Normal's toilet becomes an adapted space that changes according to the user.

 

_> Become my own attic

<Attic: An empty room, where the purpose of the space is unclear (Empty room)>. Attiq has a ‘Restroom Care System' that clears the traces of others in the current toilet and helps users' habits. Due to the corona virus, the amount of time spent at home has increased, and the amount of toilet usage has naturally increased, as well. The more the traces overlap, the more difficult it becomes for the toilet to contain individual habits. Attiq erases the traces of others that are currently overlapping in the bathroom, and creates an empty space to help change it to suit the user.

 

_> Clearing traces to

open up possibilities

Attiq helps to fill in the user's habits by creating empty spaces by erasing traces of others in the bathroom. With Attiq, your bathroom becomes an attic that you can use in your own way.

Through space dehumidification, the entire bathroom is taken care of to create an empty room, and through storage dehumidification, items that contain the user's habits are taken care of.

Daily Zone

When you look at the product, the first place that comes to you eye is the Daily Zone. The Daily Zone at the top contains items that users use every day, such as toothbrushes and razors. Since it contains items that are used every day, it is placed in the area where the user's hand can most easily reach with an easy opening and closing method.

Moment Zone

In the Moment Zone, located at the top of the product, is an easy space to store things that are placed for a while. It doesn't have to be stored for a long time, but you can keep things like a cell phone, change of clothes, headset and more.

Habit Zone

There is a Habit Zone at the bottom of the product. The Habit Zone helps to store items that are difficult to store in the bathroom or items that are not appropriate due to your original outdoor habit.

_> A sign of Etiquette

Attiq, which is used to brighten up my surroundings, lowers the light by finding the most suitable brightness for the user's eyes when the door opens. It provides etiquette to users in Attiq’s own sophisticated and manner unique way.
