_> Telecommute method for

modern creators in the new

normal

Modern creators is a profession from the new normal, which we define as people who creates and study marketers, educators, designers, planners and etc. In the new normal, the proportion of telecommute in white-collar workers such as modern creators will increase, resulting in flexible work place, working across companies, homes and other locations. What they need is a suitable working environment for each place rather than a strict one.

However, telecommuters nowadays are just satisfied with their temporal workplace, such as using their dining table as a working desk, which will eventually lead to decline of efficiency.

Shouldn’t a new adaption be needed in the new normal, where telecommuting is expanding and working environment is expanding, rather than a temporal approach?

 

_> adapth desk: Blends

into space

‘adapth’ focuses on home first among many other working conditions of modern creators. Working at home is essential to modern creators, but they don’t need as much time at home as before since they go to work and other outside places. ‘adapth desk’ can coexist without invading living place by existing in the form of partition when not working.

 

When in Ambient mode, desk naturally exists with subtle illumination.

The form frame, which reminds of a picture frame, and the fabric CMP provides a comfortable mood by harmonizing with the living place. Also, it naturally seeps into the minimal living space by using achromatic color scheme.

 

The achromatic colors not only creates minimal and comfortable mood but also coexists with the living space.

A vertical rail inside the frame operates the desk.

_> Provides suitable working

environment

‘adapth desk’ can be used as a working desk when working at home. Just slightly press the desk when it is in ambient mode, and pull forward. It will turn into a working desk. Rail-type desks allow modern creators to easily adjust to the desired angle when working on creating activities.

 

In addition, the internal cabinet which appears when used, can hold things that are required for their work such as electronic devices, writing equipment, and etc.

The magnetic storage space can be customized by user's working environment.

With its sophisticated lines and adequate proportions, ‘adapth desk’ creates a comfortable and efficient workspace when combined with living space. Creating a modern creator's work environment with a single step will be suggested as a new way of adapting to a flexible working environment at home in the new normal.

 

The rail-type adapth desk provides natural and flexible angle control.

_> adapth pad: Improve

work quality

One of the Pain points that ‘adpath’ noticed was the situation on the temporary desk of the telecommuters. Their temporary desks are disorganized without any standards, including various elect-ronic devices and notebooks. This temporary work environment can create more confusion, especially for modern creators who have a lot of creating work to do.

‘adapth pad’ unifies the In - Put roles of the three distributed devices : trackpad, tablet, and analog notes. It provides all of them as a single device.

 

1) Touchpad mode: Existing trackpad

2) Pen pad mode : Digital writing with built-in pen

3) Note mode: Analogue writing with a built-in pen(real pen tip) on a real paper placed over the pad screen. (Use of EMR Electro-magnetic Resonance Pen Technology and Digital Ink Technology)

Each side of the pen tip provides different function. One has a pen point that supports pen pad mode and the other side supports note mode.

The accompanying archiving service stores all of the document data written in ‘adapth pen’. This function can clear up the chaotic desk by even archiving analog memos that were originally easy to lose. Just take ‘adapth pad’ with you when working at other places. The integration and archiving service of ‘adapth pad’ will give you the same work experience wherever you are.

 

The archiving service stores the document data written in pen pad mode and note mode. It makes it easy to view the data anytime, anywhere.

_> Provide expanding

experience by alpha space

Even in a chaotic temporary desk environment, there were areas where work priorities converge. This is an area in front of the laptop, which can be created by just pushing the laptop. This area can be used flexibly according to your work, such as using the mouse or taking notes. ‘adapth’ defines this area as an 'alpha space', meaning the area of priority, and applied it to the desk.

 

The lines engraved on the Alpha Space will provide you with more accurate work efficiency when the pen is used.

‘adapth desk’s ‘alpha space’ is an area of 20cm from the edge of the desk. Alpha Space is linked to the ‘adapth pad’ to expand the function of the pad when using the desk. The built-in pen, which supports both pen pad mode and note mode, can be freely used in alpha space.

‘adapth’ will further enhance the work environment for modern creators by proposing a new realm of alpha space, which is based on original usability.

The adapth pad is attached with magnets in the alpha space area of the desk.

_> Suggestion for working

environment in the

new normal by adapth

Telecommuting in the new normal can be no longer simply defined as working at home. ‘adapth’ offers a new telecommu-ting environment for modern creators who will work flexibly at work, at home and outside the home.

 

‘adapth’, which is composed of ‘adapth pad’, providing three integrated In-Put modes, and ’adapth desk’, which naturally goes along with the space and provides a comfortable working environment, will be a new adaption method to the new telecommuting style in the new normal.
