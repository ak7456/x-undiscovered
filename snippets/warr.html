_> Arrangement in the time

of Same-Day Delivery

Early-morning delivery services (for fresh produces) have gained sensational popularity here in Seoul, based on the strength of ultra-convenience, time-selectable shipping system and its freshness. We no longer prefer visiting supermarket once a month to get bunch of groceries, household goods or others. Instead, we order them day by day.

The delivery itself is done by delivery man, but the real pain is to unpack, organize, and fill the fridge every morning. To end this unseen battle waged in your front door, we should reinvent the rules; rather than storing, organizing and arranging bunch of items inside your fridge, keep your produce instantly, consume it daily, and empty your storage everyday.

_> New Rules for

New Lifestyle

Refrigerators have been focusing on storing large amounts of food for a long time. But what if day-to-day consumption becomes a routine and the amount ordered at once becomes small enough to be consumed over a day or two? We need a storage that could take a totally different role.

wa.rr is a natural storage behind your front door, aiming ‘war against arrangement’. If you’re not willing to move your groceries to kitchen, just leave them at your front door. If you’re tired of cramming foods into your fridge, toss them comfortably to it. Set new rules for new lifestyle, and explore new way of living.

wa.rr can be placed anywhere between your front door and indoor gate.

_> rule 01. toss it easily

Imagine that your on your way to work, opened the front door, and faced what you've ordered last night. Are you going to take off your shoes again, go to kitchen, find an empty space in the fridge and fill them up?

wa.rr would say no to it. Instead, just toss it in comfortably to it. The wing at the top is designed to open with the force of the object falling down. The weight of the groceries are enough to open them easily, but after it passes, the spring quickly pushes it backward to keep it closed. So from now on, all you have to do is just pick up what you see and toss it on top of the wa.rr.

Isn't it easy, right?

The top wing gently swings downward, just with a light touch.

_> rule 02. No organizing

allowed

The food that passes through the wing naturally splits up due to the unique curved floor structure. For example, water bottles and cans would settle in the valley, keeping a neat shape even if you don't care. You can even toss them inside the storage without unpacking any. Whatever it is, your own way is the most natural way to use wa.rr.

Remember, no organizing allowed!

 

_> rule 03. Take out only

what you need

Have you ever ended up totally messing up your fridge, even if your first intention was to find something little? In order to prevent these chaos, we made this storage in a drawer-type structure so that you no longer have to pull everything out of your fridge to find something.

The storage space, is not deep down, so you don't have to bend your back, and is wide enough so you don't have to struggle to find what you want. Just open the drawer and take out the food you were looking for.

_> rule 04. Now, make

your own rules

If you're the one who enjoys cooking, use the carrier provided by wa.rr to pick up the ingredients you need and move them to the kitchen at once. It would be way faster than finding those hidden deep inside the fridge.

If you're taking a box of salads and coffee everyday on your way to work, just do as usual. Just before you open the front door, take out your necessaries, with your shoes on.

If you're working late at night in front of your computer, and your coffee's running out, please no longer hesitate. You don't have to walk with your heels up either. Just grab it from wa.rr. Oh, I see your coffee there.

_> Key Material:

Polycarbonate

wa.rr's polycarbonate panels are lighter than glass, but even more durable. As they are insulated by the air layer, this material is often used in inner gate or as a building enclosure.

The light projected into the air layer between panels spreads subtly to the bottom, providing a clear point on your colorless entrance. The calm presence made by lighting, materiality, and color would make this a catcher in your eye.

_> Your next mission:

War against arrangement

Why did we choose the entrance as our battlefield? That's because the entrance (right behind the front door) is the most potential space in the time of Same-Day Delivery.

As online shopping expands, the entrance is no longer a passageway, but a temporary storage for parcels and goods. People store things that they don't want to unpack right away, and even store large quantities of daily consumables, such as water and coffee, and take them out one by one.

So we decided to actively utilize the advantages of entrance. Store you food where they arrives, and make all processes to be carried out in one place without moving. Various foods can be stored here; now they could say no for being stored inside the fridge.

Everything is changing; the way we shop, the role of refrigerators, and the function of entrance. Just as a new lifestyle emerges in this new era, the environment that surrounds us must transform too. Now here is you next mission: fight against arrangement with wa.rr.
